Our Story

The Story Behind
Gordy Timeless

My name is Blessed Phiri and I've always believed that a watch says something about the person wearing it. In a world where we no longer need watches to tell time, they remain one of the few timeless symbols of discipline, taste and personal achievement.

I started Gordy Timeless to give Zambian collectors and professionals access to authentic luxury timepieces through trusted sourcing, honest advice and a personal approach to service. The name Gordy comes from my son and represents legacy, craftsmanship and stories worth passing on.
